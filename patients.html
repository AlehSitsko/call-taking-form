📌 FAQ — Patient Database


What can I do here?
This page allows you to search, view, and edit patient records. You can:
• Search by name and date of birth
• Edit details like phone, address, and notes
• Link patient data to the main call form

📝 Workflow


1. Use the search bar to find a patient.
2. Click "Edit" to pre-fill the form.
3. Modify fields and click "Save" to update.
4. New patients can be added manually.
5. Integration with main form (future feature).
